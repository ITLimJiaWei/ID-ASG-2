The Mandalorian features Pedro Pascal cast as Din Djarin, the Mandalorian. He journeys across the galaxy in hopes of protecting a particularly interesting alien, Baby Yoda. In Season 2, Din meets fan favourite characters such as Ahsoka Tano.

Grogu

Grogu or more known as "Baby Yoda" is a fan favourite in The Mandalorian and is more than what he seems. At an age of 50 years old, there is still much more to be seen about his story.

Boba Fett

Boba Fett was an infamous bounty hunter during the days of "The Empire" before being tossed in a "Sarlacc Pit" and left for dead. In "The Mandalorian" Season 2, it is revealed he is alive and wants his beskar armour back.

Din Djarin

Better known as "The Mandalorian" Din was initially part of "The Guild" before breaking its code to save Grogu from "The Empire". After being hunted relentlessly, he realised Grogu would not be safe with him and discovered that he had to bring him to one of his kind, a Jedi. In his journeys, he would discover other mandalorians with varying agendas and old friends that have changed. Needless to say, the fate of "Baby Yoda" lies in his hands.

Moff Gideon

Moff Gideon was an ISB agent on Mandalore during the time of "The Empire". By the time of "The Mandalorian" , he acquired the "Darksaber", an ancient lightsaber developed by the only Mandalorian Jedi in history, Tarre Vizsla during the time of the "Old Republic". After Din Djarin went rogue, the Moff was intent on capturing Grogu or "The Child".

Jedi Knight?After leaving the Jedi Order during The Clone Wars, she teamed up with other rebels to fight "The Empire". Djin Djarin eventually meets her on the forest moon of Corvus in the city of Calodan. There, she is on a quest to discover the location of Mitth'raw'nuruodo or more known as Grand Admiral Thrawn.

Princess Of MandaloreAfter the death of Satine Kryze and the betrayal of Darth Maul, Bo-Katan Kryze broke away from the terrorist group known as "Death Watch" and formed her own clan called the "Night Owls". Other mandalorians who believed she was the rightful heir to the Mandalorian throne continued to follow her in an attempt to retake the forsaken planet.

Mercanary and Assassin.Fennec Shand was first introduced in Season 1 of the Mandalorian and was also left for dead on Tatooine similar to Boba Fett. Boba Fett rescued her and together they tracked down Djin Djarin to retrieve his lost beskar armour. Her character is planned to return in both the live action "Book Of Boba Fett" and the animated series called "The Bad Batch".
