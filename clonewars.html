Star Wars: The Clone Wars TV Series

Star Wars: The Clone Wars takes place during after Episode I:A Phantom Menace and up to Episode III: Revenge of the Sith. It showcases the adventures of favourite Star Wars heroes such as Anakin Skywalker, Obi-Wan Kenobi while also introduces new fanfavourites such as Ahsoka Tano and Captain Rex.

Main Characters

Clone Force 99

Better known as the "Bad Batch" were a squad of specialized clone commandos part of the Grand Army of the Republic

Anakin Skywalker

Anakin Skywalker was a Jedi General during the Clone War, had a tenacity to be "unique" and cared greatly for his friends.

Obi-Wan Kenobi

Obi-Wan Kenobi was a Jedi General and was Master of Anakin Skywalker during the Clone War. He was known to adhere to the Jedi Code.

Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano was a Jedi Commander and apprentice to Anakin Skywalker. Similar to Anakin, she cared greatly about her companions.

CT-7567

Better known as Captain "Rex", CT-7567 served under General Anakin Skywalker in the Clone War and excelled in precarious situations.
