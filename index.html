Star Wars: Did you know?

The Seventh Fleet. In Mandalorian Chapter 13, the "Seventh Fleet" Insignia can be seen on the 2 HK-87 droids showcased in the episode. This would be a clue that they belonged to Grand Admiral "Thrawn" who used to command the "Seventh Fleet" before disappearing into the unknown regions with Jedi Knight, Ezra Bridger.

An iconic quote. "I got a bad feeling about this" has appeared quite often in the Star Wars Saga along with its TV Series and even video games. A main character tends to quote it whenever they are heading into a precarious situtation. Most fans recall "Han Solo" saying it in Episode 4: A New Hope before they discover the Death Star.

Creator Of Star Wars

George Lucas, born May 14, 1944 founded LucasFilm Ltd. and is best known for creating the Star Wars and Indiana Jones franchises. Though he is now semi-retired, he still contributes to Star Wars: The Mandalorian in creative design and several other aspects of cinematography.

Executive Producer

Jon Favreau contributed largely to Star Wars: The Mandalorian and was a writer for the TV series alongside Dave Filoni. Though known for his role as "Happy Hogan" in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, he is now critically acclaimed for his creative involvement in Star Wars.
