Creator Of Star Wars

George Lucas, born May 14, 1944 founded LucasFilm Ltd. and is best known for creating the Star Wars and Indiana Jones franchises. Though he is now semi-retired, he still contributes to Star Wars: The Mandalorian in creative design and several other aspects of cinematography.

Executive Producer

Jon Favreau contributed largely to Star Wars: The Mandalorian and was a writer for the TV series alongside Dave Filoni. Though known for his role as "Happy Hogan" in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, he is now critically acclaimed for his creative involvement in Star Wars.
