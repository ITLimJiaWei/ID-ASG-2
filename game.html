Star Wars: Jedi Fallen Order is a third person adventure title from Respawn Entertainment showcasing the adventures of Jedi, Cal Kestis.

Become a Jedi

Play as Cal Kestis, a Jedi that survived the great Jedi Purge and embark on an epic adventure while meeting unlikely allies and foes along the way.

Feel the Force

Reimagine the movies and use techniques such as Force Pull and Push agaisnt imperial troops across a variety of different planets.

Explore the Galaxy

Explore vast landscapes of planets new and old in the Star Wars Universe such as Zeffo, Kashyyyk and even Dathomir.
