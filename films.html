The Tragedy Of Darth Vader

Episode 1-3

From the Phantom Menace to Revenge Of The Sith, various planets and characters such as Coruscant, Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi were introduced. All at the same time, the prequels foreshadowed the fall of Anakin Skywalker and the rise of Darth Vader. In the final events of Episode III, Anakin falls to the Dark Side and Emperor Palpatine rises to power.

Rise Of The Empire

Episode 4-6

Main protagonist, Luke Skywalker, Son of Anakin Skywalker discovers he has the ability to use the Force and travels to Dagobah to be trained by Master Yoda to become a Jedi Knight. After confronting the Emperor on the second Death Star, his father turned back to the light to save Luke's life. Though, Anakin Skywalker did not live much longer after...

The First Order Arrives

Episode 7-9

After the First Order arrived from the Unknown Regions, they utilized StarKiller Base and destroyed New Republic core worlds. Rey, a scavenger from Jakku comes across Anakin Skywalker's lightsaber and trains herself across all three movies to take on the resurrected Emperor Palpatine in The Rise Of Skywalker.
